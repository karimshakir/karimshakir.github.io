My Approach To Development

  • Proper Planning

    Always plan with the end in mind. Understanding your tools, listening, and knowing the right questions to ask are crucial. Having the soft skills to connect with the customer and / or your team is an important component of success.

  • Following the Proccess

    Write code that's easy to read and understand. Use best practices. You will be loved, even if only by your future-self, while revisiting your own code.

  • Getting Involved!

    Attend meet-ups, go to capstone presentations, join coding groups. Get to know your circle beyond coding. All of these things contribute to not only being a better programmer, but a better programmer to work with!

  • Being Passionate

    Surround yourself with knowledgeable and passionate people. Strive to be that for someone else! This is what will drive you during the conundrums that lie ahead!

  • Learning!!

    Currently completing classes on Python/Django and React w/Redux
